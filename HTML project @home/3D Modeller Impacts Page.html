A 3D Modeller's Impacts

3D modellers in the film industry can help bring ideas to life. Mny movies need 3D modelling to create some characters that we now know. Almost every Hollywood film production team has 3D modellers or uses 3D modelling to speed up production and reduce costs. 3D modelling has allowed people(designers) to be able to visualize space requirements and plan accordingly. 3D models are also useful for measurements, easy re-modelling and corrections for finalizing a project. Also, scientifically, medically and educationally speaking 3D models can be made to teach and show people different things for example a hands on 3D skeletal structure through the printing of 3D data.
