What Is A 3D Modeller

By definition a 3D modeller is someone who creates a 3D representation of any surface or object in a 3D simulated space by manipulating shapes, edges and vertices. 3D modelling produces all those amazing creatures, structures,special effects, and characters that we see in many games, animations and films today.
