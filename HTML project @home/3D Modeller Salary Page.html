Salary Of A 3D Modeller

Salaries of 3D modeller's depend on their previous work experience, people the person has worked with and their skill at modelling. Someone being hired as a 3D modeller for the first time would start off as some sort of traineeAs one gets more work experience their salary might grow as well. The salary of a 3D modeller can vary depending on their specialization. The Bureau of Labour Statistics reports that 3D artists and animators got an annual median of $70 000. The top 10% of earners got $100 000 or more per year. Those in the lower 10% got as low as about %35 000 per year. The industries that paid the most to 3D modellers as of 2017 are the motion picture and video industry, followed by the software publishing industry,computer systems design, and advertising and public relations industry. These industries paid about $70 000 to $85 000. On average a 3D artist makes $53 000. That being said some 3D modeller's are in this profession as freelancers. According to the Bureau of Labour Statistics 57% of 3D modellers are self-employed. In this case salary might not be known.
